Horns

Horns are paired structures which protrude from certain animal’s skulls. They consist of a core made of live bone, which is covered by a keratinized sheath. Unlinke antlers, these structures are permanent - growing throughout an animal’s life - and do not branch. Horns also vary from between species in shape and size.
