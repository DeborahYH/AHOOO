Horns

Permanent structures that do not branch, and consist of a core made of live bone covered by a keratinized sheath. They are found in members of the Bovidae family, and their size and shape can differ between species.

Read More

Antlers

Branched structures found in members of the Cervidae family which can be shed each year and are composed of different types of tissues. While antlers can be found on female reindeer, these structures are usually found on males.
